# Research Project: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Project Overview
This repository is dedicated to researching the most effective workflows for AI-powered SEO content production in the B2B SaaS industry. The goal is to identify practical execution strategies from top industry experts to build a scalable content engine.

## Selection Criteria for Experts
I have selected 10 experts based on the following four pillars:
1. **Practical Execution:** Experts who build and share real AI workflows.
2. **Tech-Stack Diversity:** Proficiency in combining AI models (Claude, OpenAI) with automation.
3. **Innovation:** Unique approaches to Programmatic SEO and AI Agents.
4. **Community Impact:** Proven track record of high-quality, actionable insights.

## Project Structure
- `/research/sources.md`: List of experts with links and annotations.
- `/research/linkedin-posts/`: Curated insights from LinkedIn.
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/`: Deep-dives from video content.
- `/scripts/`: Tools used to gather and process this data.
