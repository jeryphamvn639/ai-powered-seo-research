After 13 years of hiring on culture fit, gut feel and "fire in the belly"… we’re changing everything.

fatjoe. is moving to a fully automated AI screening tool for every role. 🥳

I've spent thousands of hours sitting across from people trying to read between the lines of what they're saying. Trying to work out if they've got it.

BUT gut feel doesn't scale. It's slow, it's inconsistent and it relies on me being in the room.

This takes all of that off my plate.

The tool screens CVs, scores candidates against our criteria and ranks them before a human even looks at the application. What used to take me a full week now takes about 11 minutes.

But the thing I'm most happy about right now? The fact that you're still reading… which means you fell for it ;)
